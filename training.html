SKILLS

DRIBBLING

Dribbling helps you move around the court while keeping control of the ball. Good dribbling makes it easier to avoid defenders.

  • Keep the ball low and controlled
  • Use your fingertips, not your palm
  • Keep your head up while dribbling
  • Practice both left and right hand

SHOOTING

Shooting is how you score points. Proper form improves accuracy and consistency.

  • Keep your elbow straight and aligned
  • Use your legs for power
  • Follow through with your wrist
  • Aim at the rim or backboard

DEFENSE

Defense helps stop your opponent from scoring. Good defense is just as important as offense.

  • Stay low in a defensive stance
  • Keep your eyes on the opponent
  • Use your hands to block or steal
  • Move your feet quickly

PASSING

Passing helps your team move the ball efficiently and create scoring opportunities.

  • Use chest pass for quick passes
  • Use bounce pass to avoid defenders
  • Pass accurately to your teammate
  • Communicate with your team

DRILLS

DRIBBLE AROUND CONES

This drill helps improve ball control and coordination. Players dribble the ball while moving around cones in a zigzag pattern. It teaches better handling, quick direction changes, and control under movement.

WALL PASSING

This drill improves passing accuracy and strength. Players pass the ball against a wall and catch it repeatedly. It helps develop timing, control, and proper passing technique even without a partner.

FOOTWORK DRILLS

Footwork drills improve movement, balance, and speed. These drills help players move quickly, stay in control, and react better during offense and defense. Good footwork is the foundation of all basketball skills.

LAYUP PRACTICE

Layup practice helps players score close to the basket. It focuses on correct footwork, timing, and coordination. Practicing layups improves finishing skills and confidence during games.
